about us

Power GYM

It’s simple; our mission is to help you become the best version of yourself by equipping you with the knowledge and resources to reach your fitness goals. At SET FOR SET, we shy away from the bullsh*t and biases. We create fitness, health, and supplementation content that’s grounded in science-backed evidence or tried and true methodology. We aim to be your go-to resource for all your fitness and health inquiries. Since its inception over 5 years ago, SET FOR SET has left a mark on the fitness industry by introducing unconventional fitness equipment such as the Steel Mace. We then realized there was a gap in trustworthy training information online, so we set out to fill it. We’re proud to say that SET FOR SET is now one of the premier health and fitness educational resources around. Our small but mighty team comprises fitness experts, athletes, educators, dietitians, and certified personal trainers dedicated to sharing their substantial expertise with our readers. We live and die by two words, #ALWAYSREADY. At the heart of everything we do lies one truth; it’s better to be #ALWAYSREADY than get ready for when sh*t hits the fan. We hope that you join us on this journey to create stronger humans because you believe like we do, that stronger humans create stronger communities
